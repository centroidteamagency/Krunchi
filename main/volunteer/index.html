Be our Superhero
The Karunachal Volunteering Program provides a year-long
opportunity for youth (young professionals & college students) to get
real experience in the education sector and understand the on-
ground realities of the development space. The volunteer applicants
will undergo a screening process and once selected they will receive
guidance and mentoring to grow as changemakers and role models in
the community by developing their project management, critical
thinking and leadership skills.
Volunteer Roles
Community Leaders (Public Relations)
Coordinate with practitioners & experts from the field related to environment, entrepreneurship or
exploration.
Time commitment: 6 Months, 6 Hours/week, Weekdays
Camp Champs (Operations)
Manage back-end operations for smooth execution of experiential learning camps.
Time commitment: 12 Months, 6 Hours/week, Weekdays
Charismatic Doers (Fundraising)
Dream, Develop, Design & Execute exciting fundraising events themed on environment,
entrepreneurship and exploration.
Time commitment: 6 Months, 8 Hours/week, Weekends
Creative Heroes (Human Resource)
Manage communication, marketing, sales, logistics and hospitality/experience for experiential
learning camps.
Time commitment: 6 Months, 6 Hours/week, Weekdays
Currency Experts (Finance)
Manage coordination of finances for smooth functioning of financial transactions within the
organisation.
Time commitment: 12 Months, 6 Hours/week, Weekends
